DSP TTS : Double-Scale Prosody TTS Model For Chinese Speech Synthesize

    • Overall Structure of DSP-TTS

    Abstract:

      Text-to-speech (TTS) aims to generate natural speech from provided text. Expressive speech synthesis greatly aids in synthesizing natural voices. Previous works on expressive Chinese TTS system focus more on the diffiences of the grammatical system between English and Chinese, however, the pronunication systems between both two languages are also divergent. The prosody in Chinese could be divided into intra-prosody and inter-prosody, and intra-prosody indicates the rhythmic variation within each phoneme duration, if the two are not modelled in harmony it can lead to unnatural speech. Therefore, we propose DSP-TTS with double-scale prosody extractors and predictors to capture inter-word and intra-word prosody respectively and joint training to uniform modelling of these two prosody. Both objective and subjective evaluations on the Mandarin Speech Dataset BZNSP demonstrate that the proposed method significantly improve the naturalness and expressiveness of the Chinese synthesized speech.
